Welcome to The Spooky Bookshelf!

Hello and welcome to The Spooky Bookshelf! This is my little corner of the indie web where you will find a variety of things - book reviews, author interviews, crafts, and more. This page will serve as my personal homepage and my book blog, so be ready for just about anything!

A Little About Me!

Hi there! I'm Charli. I'm a Christian, cybersecurity & computer networking student, bookworm, metalhead, horror lover, crafter, and front-end web developer.
